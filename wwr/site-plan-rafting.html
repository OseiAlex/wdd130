Overview

Purpose

Exciting news! We've embarked on a thrilling journey to establish our online presence, bringing the heart-pounding excitement of white-water rafting straight to your fingertips. Our decision to dive into the digital realm is fueled by a commitment to providing an immersive and seamless experience for both seasoned adventurers and those taking their first plunge into the world of rafting.

Audience

Our target customers are individuals aged 25-40 who are passionate about sustainable living, eco-friendly products, and a healthy lifestyle. They are environmentally conscious and seek ways to make a positive impact on the planet through their choices. Many of them are well-educated professionals with a moderate to high financial capacity to invest in sustainable practices.
They want access to a platform that curates information, products, and tips for sustainable living. Our customers are interested in discovering eco-friendly alternatives for everyday products, staying informed about environmental issues, and adopting practices that align with their values. They seek a sense of community and shared values with others who are committed to making environmentally conscious choices.
When accessing our website, they expect to find a well-organized hub of information on sustainable living. This includes guides on reducing carbon footprint, product reviews for eco-friendly alternatives, and articles on the latest advancements in green technology. They anticipate a user-friendly interface that encourages exploration and discovery, fostering a sense of community engagement.
While there are sources providing information on sustainability, our target customers often find it challenging to navigate through the vast amount of available content. They desire a platform that not only educates but also simplifies the process of adopting sustainable practices in their daily lives. A lack of curated, trustworthy recommendations for eco-friendly products and a sense of community is a gap we aim to fill. Our customers need a reliable source that understands their values and provides actionable steps towards a more sustainable lifestyle.
