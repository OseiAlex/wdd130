Welcome to Rapids Rafting!

Rapids Rafting Company, founded in 1990 by outdoor enthusiasts Sarah and Michael Thompson, is a premier provider of exhilarating white water rafting experiences. Situated in the picturesque valley of Clearwater, Colorado, Rapids Rafting Company has established itself as a leading adventure tourism destination, offering unforgettable journeys down some of the region's most thrilling rivers.
With decades of experience and a team of seasoned guides, Rapids Rafting Company offers a diverse range of rafting trips tailored to suit various preferences and skill levels. From leisurely family floats along gentle currents to adrenaline-pumping descents through Class IV rapids, our excursions cater to both novice rafters seeking a taste of adventure and seasoned paddlers in search of new challenges.

Our History

Rapid Adventures, founded in 1985 by avid river enthusiast John Smith, emerged as a trailblazer in the realm of white water rafting. Smith's passion for adventure, coupled with his deep appreciation for the natural beauty of river landscapes, spurred the inception of the company.
Initially operating on a small scale, Rapid Adventures began with humble roots, offering guided rafting trips down local rivers. As the company gained momentum, its reputation for delivering thrilling and safe excursions grew exponentially.

Over the years, Rapid Adventures expanded its repertoire to include a diverse array of rafting experiences, catering to both novice adventurers and seasoned thrill-seekers. From leisurely family-friendly floats to adrenaline-pumping Class V rapids, the company's offerings spanned the spectrum of river experiences.
Driven by a commitment to environmental stewardship and responsible tourism, Rapid Adventures actively engaged in conservation efforts aimed at preserving the pristine ecosystems that served as the backdrop for their expeditions.
